Environmental Action FAQs

How do I report illegal deforestation in my area? +

Use our Crisis Map to upload photos, GPS coordinates, and details. All reports are anonymized by default and forwarded to local environmental agencies.

What evidence do I need to document pollution? +

Capture clear photos/videos of the pollution source (e.g., factory pipes, dumped waste), location data, and timestamps. If safe, note any identifying details (license plates, company logos).

Can corporations see who reported them? +

No. We comply with whistleblower protection laws. Your identity remains confidential unless you choose to disclose it for legal cases.

How are my reports used to create change? +

Reports are:
1. Mapped publicly to raise awareness
2. Sent to regulators for enforcement
3. Used by NGOs to prioritize cleanups
4. Featured in our annual Environmental Accountability Report.
